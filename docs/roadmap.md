- Define product thesis
- Define lead assistant soul

- Broader reminders and routines
- ongoing thread tracking
- richer personal memory
- research workflows

## Phase 4: Interfaces

- Telegram or Slack channel
- minimal dashboard
- timeline of conversations, briefs, and market events
