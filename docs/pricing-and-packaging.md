# Pricing And Packaging

## Recommended Model

Sell Aether as a **managed single-tenant client deployment**.

Do not sell:

- source code ownership
- one-time perpetual handoff
- broad white-label rights by default

Sell:

- setup and onboarding
- private hosted access
- branded deployment
- ongoing support and iteration

## Offer Structure

Use a three-part commercial model:

1. setup fee
2. monthly platform fee
3. optional customization / advisory fee

This protects the product and keeps value in:

- deployment
- maintenance
- workflow tuning
- support
- ongoing iteration

## Suggested Pricing

### Tier 1: Private Desk

For solo discretionary traders.

- Setup: `$2,500`
- Monthly: `$600-$1,000`

Includes:

- one private deployment
- your branding
- desk, threads, journal, watchlist, tasks, routines
- Telegram integration
- basic onboarding
- reasonable support

Best for:

- serious solo traders
- high-income retail traders
- traders already journaling or planning manually

### Tier 2: Premium Operator

For higher-touch clients who want customization and active iteration.

- Setup: `$5,000-$8,000`
- Monthly: `$1,500-$3,000`

Includes:

- everything in Private Desk
- branded wrapper and refined client presentation
- workflow customization
- routine tuning
- prompt/memory refinement
- higher-touch support
- periodic review / iteration

Best for:

- profitable discretionary traders
- mentors
- boutique trading operators

### Tier 3: White-Label Client Edition

For educators, coaches, and boutique brands.

- Setup / license: `$12,000-$30,000+`
- Monthly support / platform fee: `$2,000-$5,000+`

Includes:

- branded client edition
- custom naming, wrapper, and packaging
- controlled white-label rights
- deployment support
- ongoing maintenance contract

Best for:

- coaches with paid clients
- boutique trading brands
- high-ticket research communities

## Pricing Logic

The pricing is justified by:

- workflow value, not token cost
- private deployment, not commodity access
- operational memory and continuity
- reduced process friction
- premium niche buyer profile

Do not price this like:

- a chatbot subscription
- a retail indicator
- a $49/month SaaS tool

That anchors it too low and attracts the wrong customer.

## First Deals Recommendation

For the first 3 to 5 clients:

- charge enough to keep positioning strong
- leave room for “founding client” flexibility

Suggested founding-client structure:

- Setup: `$1,500-$3,000`
- Monthly: `$500-$1,200`

Use this only to:

- get real users
- get usage feedback
- collect testimonials / case studies
- refine the product

Do not stay at founding pricing too long.

## Optional Add-Ons

Add-ons you can price separately:

- extra branding and wrapper work
- custom routines
- tailored trading workflows
- additional channel integrations
- monthly review / advisory calls
- migration of existing notes / journal structure

Suggested add-on pricing:

- small customization: `$500-$1,500`
- medium customization: `$2,000-$5,000`
- strategic advisory / review retainer: `$500-$2,000` monthly

## Payment Model

Use:

- setup fee paid upfront
- monthly fee billed in advance
- custom work billed by scoped fixed fee

Avoid:

- unpaid pilots
- vague “let’s see how it goes” builds
- source-delivery deals disguised as setup work

## Positioning Lines

Short version:

> Aether is a private operator surface for discretionary traders who need more
> continuity, discipline, and memory than a normal chat tool can provide.

Premium version:

> We deploy a branded private trading operator for each client: one surface for
> planning, threads, journal capture, watchlist management, desk routines, and
> chart context.

## Commercial Guardrails

Always separate:

- client data ownership
- software ownership

The client should own:

- their data
- their notes
- their journal content

You should own:

- the codebase
- the product IP
- the deployment framework
- the reusable workflows and wrapper
