The problem

You already know what good trading looks like. The issue is continuity: your context resets every session, your reasoning scatters across apps, and no tool remembers what you were thinking yesterday.

Plans in one app, charts in another, reflections in a third. Nothing connects.

Journaling dies on friction

You know you should journal. You start, then drift. If it's not embedded in the workflow, it doesn't happen.

Zero memory

Every session starts from scratch. Your reasoning, thesis, and market read evaporate overnight.

Most traders don't lose edge because they lack information. They lose edge because their thinking resets — fragments across apps, evaporates between sessions, and never compounds into the kind of pattern recognition that separates consistency from noise.
