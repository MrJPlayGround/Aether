Aether / Private AI Trading Agent

A private AI trading agent that remembers how you think.

Aether gives serious discretionary traders one private system for chat, memory, desk state, journal capture, chart context, watchlist discipline, tasks, and routines. It is decision support with continuity, not another chat window that forgets everything overnight.
